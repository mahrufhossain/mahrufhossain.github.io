About Me

Hello, I'm Mahruf Hossain, an Electrical and Electronic Engineering undergrad with a deep interest in how things work — and how to build them better.

I have a strong interest in circuit design, both digital and analog, and I enjoy working on PCBs and embedded systems. I like the hands-on side of engineering — turning ideas into real, working hardware.

Whether it's sketching out a circuit or writing code, I find joy in the process of creating and learning. I'm always exploring new tools, trying new approaches, and building things that make sense.
